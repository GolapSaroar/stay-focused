# Focus Space: Your Mind, Reclaimed ✦

Focus Space is a beautiful, distraction-free productivity dashboard designed to help you reclaim your time from the feed. It operates entirely as a single-page application with zero external dependencies, meaning it loads instantly and runs entirely in your browser.
visit: https://GolapSaroar.github.io/stay-focused/
## 🎯 Features

* **Pomodoro Timer**: Includes built-in 25-minute focus, 5-minute break, and 50-minute deep work modes to structure your sessions.
* **Breathing Tool**: Features a 12-second guided breathing ring (4s inhale, 2s hold, 4s exhale, 2s hold) to help kill the urge to scroll.
* **Distraction Logging**: Allows you to log and track your distraction triggers (like Instagram, YouTube, or Twitter) to build awareness.
* **Task & Goal Management**: A simple checklist for today's goals and a dedicated text area for your overarching daily intention.
* **Energy Tracking**: A mood grid to log your current mental state, from "Wired 🔥" to "Tired 😴".
* **Local Persistence**: All your data, stats, and goals are saved securely in your browser's local storage—no database required.
* **Exportable Data**: Generates a downloadable `.txt` report of your daily focus stats, completed goals, and distraction logs.
